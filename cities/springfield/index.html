Springfield

“A noble spirit embiggens the smallest man.” Founded in 1796 by Jebediah Springfield, a.k.a. Hans Sprungfeld, and a band of fiercely determined pioneers out of Maryland who set off after misinterpreting a passage in The Bible, Springfield seems to be a small city rather than a megalopolis. Its founder is not confined by bureaucracy or business as usually. It's 100% out-of-the-box thinking in Springfield.
