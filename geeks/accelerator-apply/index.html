The Opportunity

The $172 billion government IT spending market is ripe and ready for disruption. It needs new tools and technologies that provide high value at reasonable costs — and it needs new entrepreneurs and businesses to provide them. The Code for America Accelerator is looking for civic-focused startups and entrepreneurs to do just this.

Code for America partners with local government to use modern technology tools and approaches to foster collaboration with their community, improve internal government processes and service delivery, and more effectively and efficiently address community needs. Startups are a crucial part of creating governments that work for the people, by the people, in the 21st century.

We already knew that we were disrupting the state of technology for a specific pain point, and the Accelerator helped us realize the part we can play to transform government into a platform on which vendors like us, citizens, and public sector employees work together to improve society.

Benefits

  • Training and mentorship: World-class content and mentorship from CfA’s extensive network of leaders in the technology and public administration space. Last year, mentors spent over 200 hours with the companies, on topics such as sales, lead generation, UX, marketing, product and fundraising.
  • Funding: Startups receive $25,000 USD per team to grow their businesses. As is customary with many Accelerators, our investment stake will depend on the stage and structure of the company, and will be determined prior to acceptance of any company in the Accelerator.
  • Office space: Startups have access to our coworking space and meeting rooms. Additionally, every month for the course of the program, you'll convene at Code for America HQ in downtown San Francisco for a week of mentorship, meetings and training. Many teams travel back and forth between SF and their hometowns for the duration of this program.
  • Services and support: Your team will gain special access to product credits and training from some of our donors and sponsors
  • Network and publicity: Access to CfA’s extensive government network, including an opportunity to present, as well as participate in the Civic Tech Fair at the 2014 CfA Summit. Your team will receive 3 tickets to the Summit, which included over 80 governments in 2013.
