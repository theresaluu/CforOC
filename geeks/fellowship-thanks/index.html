Thanks for Applying

Thank you for applying for the Code for America fellowship. You’re all done with Step One of the application process -- you should receive a confirmation note shortly at the email address you provided.

You and hackers like you are the vanguard of a new wave of citizenship. We salute you. While our Fellowship is a unique program, it’s far too small to accommodate all the great people who want to use their skills to make a difference. But there many ways to lend your skills. Whether you’re ultimately selected as a Fellow or not, we hope civic hacking will be a lifelong pursuit.

What to Do Next

Please join us at an event, contribute code to one of our projects, or participate in the brigade.

Now, go tell the world: “I just applied to @CodeforAmerica! http://c4a.me/evpEmz” and we’ll be in touch about the next steps in mid-late August.

We hope to see you soon, at a meeting or online. Keep hacking!
