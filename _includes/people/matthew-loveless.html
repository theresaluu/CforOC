Matthew Loveless

Data & Analytics Manager

Member of

Matthew Loveless joined Code for America in 2014. Before CfA, Matthew worked on data integration and management at the U.S. Department of Energy, the Democratic National Committee, and Barack Obama’s 2008 presidential campaign. At CfA, Matthew helps to track organizational growth, keep the data house in order, and assess program impacts. Matthew is originally from Seattle, and first came to the Bay Area to attend Stanford University. After several years in Washington, DC, he now lives in Glen Park and bakes a lot of bread.
