Becca Blazak

Program Coordinator

Member of

  • Staff

Becca joined Code for America in May 2014. As Programs Coordinator, Becca works across all five of Code for America’s programs, helping to further institutionalize program integration. Becca comes from the Skoll Foundation where she worked in a similar role, providing support and helping develop projects such as the Young Leader Initiative and Corporate Experience for the 2014 Skoll World Forum. Before moving to the Bay Area, Becca spent 15 months teaching English in South Korea and backpacking around Southeast Asia. While in South Korea, she was the co-creator of an after school English program for elementary aged girls and Girls Town Orphanage. Becca holds a B.A. in Culture and Communications with a focus in International Studies and a minor in Politics from Ithaca College.
